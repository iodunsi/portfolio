Overview

For this project, my group was tasked with researching a concept for conducting usability testing for a SAAS Healthcare Application. We found that common pain points included system defaults, data entry issues, and missing health alert notifications.

The rest of our findings and recommendations can be found in the presentation below.

Reflection

This project strengthened my skills in identifying user pain points within complex systems and communicating findings clearly to stakeholders. It emphasized the importance of usability testing in healthcare, where even small design issues can have a major impact on patient safety and user efficiency.
