Overview

This project builds on a website I created for my Content Management Systems class, where we developed an AI news site using Drupal. I focused on the frontend design, which was evaluated based on how closely it matched a Figma prototype my teammate Jay and I created.

Later, for a five-minute presentation in my Professional Development in Computing class, I critiqued and reflected on that frontend design.

Reflection

What went well

  • Design archetypes appealed to “The Explorer”
  • Included testimonials & strong calls-to-action
  • Fluid design, easy to navigate

What could be improved

  • Lacked mobile responsiveness (Was only graded on how closely frontend resembled Figma prototype, so I overlooked this.)
  • More vibrant color scheme could have been used to add life to the webpage and foster user engagement.
