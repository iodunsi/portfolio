My Very First UX Project

Designing a Lost & Found App for the NJIT community

The Lost Initiative

The Lost Initiative was a collaborative project that focused on designing a Lost and Found App for the NJIT community. The process included journey and empathy maps, user personas, structured interviews, and prototyping (low to high fidelity).

View Full Documentation

Design Process

Research

Interviews, surveys, and user personas to guide design decisions.

Prototyping

Low-fidelity sketches to high-fidelity screens in Proto.io.

Testing

Usability testing sessions with peers, iterating based on feedback.

Reflection

This project was my first introduction to UX, and I really enjoyed learning about user-centered design. The iterative process gave me valuable insights into accessibility, usability, and collaboration.
